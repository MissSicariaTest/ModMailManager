## 2. Description of the App

ModMailManager is a Reddit Devvit application that forwards modmail messages, new post submissions, and mod queue alerts from your subreddit to Discord channels via webhooks. Optional features include interactive Discord ticket management buttons and daily moderation reports.

## 4. Data

- The App processes modmail messages, post submissions, and mod queue events from your subreddit and forwards them to the Discord webhook URLs you configure.

- No data is sold or shared with third parties beyond the Discord channels you configure.
